## The discipline

Three rules apply across every claim made in this work:

1. **Pre-register the falsifier before the test.** Every load-bearing claim gets a pre-registered hypothesis + falsifier, recorded verdict, and tally against Type-I (overclaim) / Type-II (missed-risk) catches.
2. **Pre-1.0 vocabulary until the substrate earns the words.** Zig itself is pre-1.0; no v1.x semver claim on a Zig substrate until Zig 1.0 ships.
3. **Honest non-claims.** Every artifact ships with an explicit "what does NOT ship yet" section.
